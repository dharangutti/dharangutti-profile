These independently authored white papers explore emerging engineering concepts across energy systems, resilient electronics, and technology architecture. Selected papers are archived on Zenodo with citable DOIs. They are developed as personal technical research and are not associated with or representative of any employer or client.

Archived on Zenodo (DOI)

  • Flux Logic Computing
    18 July 2026 · Working paper · v2.0 · CC BY 4.0

    A conceptual digital logic family in which binary information is represented by deterministic magnetic flux patterns rather than voltage ranges. Defines two canonical flux states, a gate abstraction based on pattern recognition and Boolean evaluation, and a propagation model, with explicit axioms and success criteria for future validation.

  • Seabed-Anchored Adaptive Ocean Energy Network
    15 July 2026 · Working paper · v1.0 · CC BY 4.0

    A conceptual marine-energy architecture placing power-take-off, generator, and grid-forming infrastructure on the seabed while lightweight hydrodynamic collectors operate on controlled high-tension tethers. Covers operating modes, PTO options, control architecture, engineering constraints, and a staged validation pathway from analytical modelling to open-sea demonstrators.
